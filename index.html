About

Hi, I'm Maansi! My journey into data science began with a stroke of luck – winning an iPad on a scratchie at a New Zealand airport. It sparked my curiosity about technology, and I was captivated by the endless possibilities this device offered, sparking a desire for more. Pursuing Computer Science seemed like the obvious choice, but it left me wanting more. Then, in my final year, Machine Learning reignited my passion for its potential to solve complex problems. Hungry for real-world impact, I joined Plaksha's Tech Leaders fellowship, honing my skills and boosting my confidence. Fueled by a passion for data's transformative power, I am actively seeking opportunities to explore its applications across diverse domains. My experience in projects ranging from customer sentiment analysis in retail to drone-based crop health and yield assessment in agriculture honed my ability to adapt my skills to varying industry needs. I'm excited to leverage this versatility to make a real-world impact through data-driven solutions.
